You're all set

Your WhatCable Pro licence key is on its way to your inbox. Check your email and follow these steps to activate.

How to activate

1
Check your email for a message from noreply@whatcable.uk with your licence key.
2
Right-click WhatCable in the menu bar and click Licence.
3
Paste your key into the licence field and activate.
4
Pro features unlock immediately. Works on up to 2 Macs.

No email after a few minutes? Check your spam folder or contact support.
